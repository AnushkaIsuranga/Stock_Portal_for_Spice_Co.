About Us

At Dilthira Associates, we pride ourselves on our significant role in the Sri Lankan spice trading landscape, with a specialization in premium black pepper. Our journey began in 2010 as a pepper powder stock trading company, driven by our vision to provide top-quality products at affordable prices.

Over the years, we have evolved into a renowned name in the industry, particularly catering to the needs of top-tier hotels and restaurants in Kandy. Our commitment to delivering high-quality pepper powder products at accessible prices has garnered widespread recognition among our valued customers and fellow businesses. We are honored by the trust and confidence placed in us by the market, and we remain dedicated to upholding our standards of excellence in quality affordability.
